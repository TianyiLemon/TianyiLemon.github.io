On the Complexity of Something Interesting

Yipin Wang, Collaborator One, Collaborator Two

Conference on Very Theoretical Things (CVTT 2025)

We prove that for any graph $G = (V, E)$ with treewidth at most $k$, the problem of computing $\chi(G)$ admits an algorithm running in time $O(2^k \cdot n)$, improving upon the previous bound of $O(3^k \cdot n^2)$.

A Note on Approximating Max-Cut

Yipin Wang

Preprint, 2024

We present a simplified analysis of the Goemans-Williamson algorithm, achieving the approximation ratio of $\alpha_{GW} \approx 0.878$ using elementary techniques from convex optimization.
